Best Movies According To Akhila

My Top 3 Movies

1. Sita Ramam

This is my all time favourite movie. I love how the characters have been built. There is A Princess who left her kingdom to live with her king, love of her life. And there is a solider who sacrificed his own life for his country. The best part is, he knew that the entire nation would blam him but not his Sita. And she did as he thought. Afreen is a pak citizen who hates india turned to be the one helping 18 prisoners of india in her country after knewing Ram deeds. A movie with amazing storywriting, music and every single thing is on point which makes the audience to feel the raw emotions of love, sacrifice, patriotism and many more. I can watch this movie again and again without getting bored.

2. Karthikeya 2

This movie explores the roots of our culture.A story where Shree Krishna's kada is necessery for human live surviving, karthik is the one who reachs Shree Krishna's kada while facing evil mind people and nature who tests him if he is real the one worth to reach this place. I love how they showed that one a pure hearted person can reach Shree Krishna's warmth. Karthik is a doctor who doesn't blindly belive in god, is actually the one choosen for this advanture created by Shree Krishna himself. The screenplay is amazing while how karthik faced and overcame those challenges to reach the goal.

3. Seethamma vakitlo sirimalle chettu

This is a family movie which shows the importance of family and the authentic feel of wedding in villages.I love that marriage scenes where all the family members got invloved with love. They showed the difference between pedhodu and chinnodu while showing there pure bond too. It's like a weekend movie for families out there in AP(Telugu States). I love how they showed the bonding between a joint family.
